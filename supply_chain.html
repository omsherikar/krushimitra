Supply Chain Management

What is Supply Chain Management?

Supply chain management involves the management of the flow of goods and services. It includes all processes that transform raw materials into finished products.

Key Components of Supply Chain

  • Supplier Management
  • Inventory Management
  • Logistics and Transportation
  • Production Planning
  • Customer Service

Benefits of Efficient Supply Chain

Improved efficiency, reduced operational costs, enhanced customer satisfaction, and better decision-making.
