Financial Planning

Introduction to Financial Planning

Financial planning is the process of managing your finances to achieve personal economic satisfaction. It involves creating a roadmap for saving, investing, and spending wisely.

Steps in Financial Planning

  1. Assess your current financial situation.
  2. Set financial goals (short, medium, and long term).
  3. Create a budget and manage your expenses.
  4. Invest wisely for future growth.
  5. Review and adjust your plan regularly.

Important Aspects of Financial Planning

  • Budgeting and saving
  • Insurance and risk management
  • Retirement planning
  • Tax planning
  • Estate planning
