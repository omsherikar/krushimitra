Knowledge of Farming

Introduction to Farming

Farming is the practice of cultivating soil, growing crops, and raising animals for food, fiber, medicinal plants, and other products used to sustain and enhance human life.

Types of Farming

  • Arable farming: Growing crops like wheat, rice, etc.
  • Pastoral farming: Raising animals like cattle, sheep, etc.
  • Mixed farming: Combination of both arable and pastoral farming.

Best Farming Practices

Implement sustainable farming methods such as crop rotation, agroforestry, and organic farming to promote soil health and minimize environmental impact.
